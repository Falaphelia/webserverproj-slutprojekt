  • A quick overview:
    • To use the to-do list, you first need an account:
    • Create an account in account registration. It is linked above.
    • Login to your newly created account in the login screen.
  • If you are logged in, then great! Now go to To-Do and we'll continue from there:
    • To create a list, enter its desired name under "Create New List" and press the button "Create List".
    • To delete a list, click on the big green delete button. This button also states the name of the list, make sure you are deleting the correct list!
    • To create an entry to your list, define its name and description under "Add Entry" and click "Add Entry".
    • Done with your entry and want to get rid of it? Simply click the green delete button to the right!
